DECART
DOMESTIC CASE ESTIMATES FROM CASES AMONG RETURNING TRAVELLERS

This tool estimates the number of cases of an epidemic in a source region, from the number of cases detected by non-resident travellers who visited that source region. The method needs population sizes for the source regions, numbers of visitors into those source regions, and the mean duration of stay. Even with a small number of detected cases, this simple approach can yield useful estimates. For examples of the method in action, see the papers below:-
